# Module 01 Quick Pass

## 5 Explicit Requirements

1. Learners can browse providers by category.
2. Learners can view provider profiles with ratings.
3. Learners can book provider time slots through the platform.
4. Learners can pay for booked sessions through the platform.
5. Providers can set their own availability, pricing, and service descriptions.

## 2 Ambiguities

1. "Search should feel instant" is a quality target, but the spec does not define the response-time threshold or load profile.
2. "Provider cancellation policy applied" implies provider-specific rules, but the spec does not say whether those policies are structured rules, free text, or platform-approved templates.

## 1 Question For The PM

1. Does the fixed 15% platform commission apply to every booking in every city and service category, or are there commission exceptions we need to model now?
